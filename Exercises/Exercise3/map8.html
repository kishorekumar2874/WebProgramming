Uranus

Uranus is the seventh planet from the Sun and is known for its unique blue-green color. It rotates on its side, making it distinct among the planets. Uranus is an ice giant, with a composition including water, methane, and ammonia. It has a faint ring system and at least 27 known moons. Uranus has a very cold atmosphere, with temperatures dropping as low as -224°C (-371°F).
